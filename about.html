Founders

Nick, co-founder of AxN, has been building a brand and websites since beginning his freelance creative work. He has shot commercials for million dollar companies, managed funds for local companies, and designed work. Axn is his hub for his work and to connect with potential clients. Furthermore, Nick has had several hours of experience in coding and is furthering his scope of code by learning new coding languages such as java script.

Austin, co-founder of AxN, is in partnership with Nick for building their brand. Austin aims to utilize AxN as a means to exhibit his personal work, which ranges from electronic builds and guides to product reviews and specifications. His end goal is to expand AxN to a community for all creatives to showcase their talents. Austin has had several collage offers for computer sciences and software development.
